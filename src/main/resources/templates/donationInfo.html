Why Donate to Bible-it


Bible-it donations are used to:


Keep the service available to everyone around the world

As the service and demand on the system grows, more powerful hardware and infrastructures are required to keep up with the stress the system could face. Funds from the donations will be used to support these new demands.

Bring the Bible-it service to as many countries and languages possible

The word of God is immeasurable in value and is a guide to salvation. Funds from donations will be used to bring the service to as many nations and langues possible, with the best quality God allows for.

Continually develop and enhance current and new features to improve the Bible-it service

Bible-it at its core brings answers to relevant questions that Christ followers and others who want to know of Gods word may have. Funds from donations will be used to support the people who develop, improve, inspect, and validate the information presented by this service, guided by the Holy Spirit through the name of Jesus Christ our Lord.

Implement new and top technologies for the use of God's kingdom

Technology helps bring information to the entire world, it is vastly impressive and has great potential to be used for good. Funds from donations will be used to guide and implement top technologies for the service of God and his kingdom.


Thank you for your consideration and donations to Bible-it. God bless you.
